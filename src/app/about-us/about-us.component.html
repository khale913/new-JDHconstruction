24-Hour Emergency Cleanup & Restoration

JDH Construction is a property damage restoration company that provides emergency disaster cleanup and restoration services, day or night. We specialize in properties that result in damage from water, fire, smoke, mold, weather, and many others. Our top priority is making sure that you get back your home that you had pre-disaster. Feel free to contact us anytime, 24/7, to get an assessment with one of our professional technicians.

At JDH Construction, we know that your biggest concern is getting back into your home as fast and safely as possible. We work with our property owners to make sure they feel as comfortable as possible with the restoration services being provided.
